About Me

After graduating from San Jose State University with my B.S. in Chemical Engineering, I worked as a safety engineer at a cleanroom production facility. I lost interest in the work due to my minimal impact to the company's culture, so I started considering alternative career options in 2021. I figured I would dip my toes in a multitude of options over the course of 2022, but after starting with web development, I never thought to look elsewhere. As time has progressed, I've applied myself wholly to the craft of software engineering. My current technologies are mentioned in my resume, but I've been enjoying learning new technologies and frameworks as they become necessary. I really enjoy working on teams as well as working alone, and I am looking forward to my future as a software engineer.
